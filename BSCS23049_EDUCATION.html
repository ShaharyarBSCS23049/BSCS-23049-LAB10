    As stated previously, i started my educational journey at the age of 3. I did my O-levels from Brick School. My o-levels involed me studying the regular 5 subjects compulsory for pakistan and the optional ICT, Physics and Chemistry. I did my A-levels from Lahore Alma. It was quite a drastic rise in difficulty. The subjects i studied were Computer Science, Mathematics and physics. Bricks was a better experience then Alma academically, my life there was also more fun. My educational experience under the cambridge system was exceptional, this system offered less rote learning and more conceptual studies then our traditional system.
